Daycare & After-School Programs

Flexible Childcare Solutions for Working Parents

At Evergreen, we understand the challenges faced by working parents in balancing professional responsibilities with quality childcare. Our daycare and after-school programs are designed to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for children while parents are at work.

With extended hours from early morning to evening, our flexible scheduling options cater to various family needs, ensuring that your child receives consistent care and engaging activities throughout the day.

Our Daycare Services

Full-Day Daycare (Ages 2-6)

Our comprehensive daycare program provides all-day care for preschool-aged children, combining educational activities with play, rest, and meals in a structured daily routine.

Program Features:

  • Extended hours from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM
  • Nutritious breakfast, lunch, and snacks
  • Age-appropriate educational activities
  • Supervised indoor and outdoor play
  • Quiet time and rest periods
  • Regular progress updates for parents

Schedule Options:

  • Full-week (Monday to Friday)
  • Part-week (2-3 days per week)
  • Flexible drop-in (subject to availability)

After-School Program (Ages 5-12)

Our after-school program provides a supportive environment for school-aged children to complete homework, engage in enrichment activities, and enjoy supervised recreation after regular school hours.

Program Features:

  • School pickup service from selected nearby schools
  • Supervised homework completion time
  • Nutritious afternoon snacks
  • Enrichment activities (arts, crafts, music, etc.)
  • Indoor and outdoor games and recreation
  • Special interest clubs and activities

Schedule:

Monday to Friday: 3:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Extended hours available on school holidays and early dismissal days

Holiday Camps

During school holidays and breaks, we offer special camp programs filled with fun activities, field trips, and themed projects to keep children engaged and entertained.

Camp Features:

  • Full-day program (8:00 AM - 5:30 PM)
  • Weekly themes and special projects
  • Arts, crafts, and creative activities
  • Sports and outdoor adventures
  • Educational workshops and experiments
  • Local field trips and special guests

Available During:

  • Summer vacation
  • Diwali break
  • Winter holidays
  • Other school holidays

Nutrition and Meals

We provide nutritious, balanced meals and snacks prepared fresh daily in our kitchen. Our menu is designed to meet children's nutritional needs while introducing them to a variety of healthy foods.

Our Approach to Nutrition:

  • Balanced meals with appropriate portions for different age groups
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables served daily
  • Limited sugar and processed foods
  • Accommodation for food allergies and dietary restrictions
  • Weekly menu shared with parents in advance

Parents are welcome to provide meals from home if preferred, especially for children with specific dietary needs or preferences.
