Frequently Asked Questions

What are your admission requirements?

Our admission process involves completing an application form, submitting required documents (birth certificate, immunization records), and scheduling a visit to our facility. We accept children based on availability in the appropriate age group.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes, we provide nutritious meals and snacks for children enrolled in our full-day programs. Our menu is designed to meet children's nutritional needs and includes fresh fruits, vegetables, and balanced meals. We can accommodate special dietary requirements with advance notice.

What is your teacher-to-child ratio?

We maintain low teacher-to-child ratios to ensure quality care and individual attention. Our ratios vary by age group: 1:4 for Playgroup (2-3 years), 1:6 for Nursery (3-4 years), 1:8 for Jr. KG (4-5 years), and 1:10 for Sr. KG (5-6 years).

What security measures do you have in place?

Child safety is our top priority. Our security measures include controlled access to the building, CCTV surveillance, secure pickup procedures (authorized pickup list), regular safety drills, and staff trained in first aid and emergency procedures.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we offer transportation services for our after-school program from selected nearby schools. For our regular preschool programs, transportation can be arranged based on location and availability for an additional fee.
