Our Programs

Early Childhood Education Programs

At Evergreen Preschool, we offer age-appropriate educational programs designed to nurture young minds and prepare them for future academic success. Our curriculum balances structured learning with play-based activities to develop cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills.

Each program is tailored to meet the developmental needs of children at different stages, ensuring a smooth transition from one level to the next. Our experienced educators create a supportive environment where children can explore, discover, and grow at their own pace.

Playgroup (2-3 years)

Our Playgroup program is designed for toddlers taking their first steps into a structured learning environment. The focus is on developing basic social skills, language acquisition, and motor skills through guided play and exploration.

Program Highlights:

  • Gentle separation from parents and adaptation to school routine
  • Development of language and communication skills
  • Fine and gross motor skill activities
  • Sensory exploration and creative play
  • Music, movement, and storytelling sessions
  • Basic self-help skills and independence

Nursery (3-4 years)

The Nursery program builds on the foundation established in Playgroup, introducing more structured learning activities while continuing to emphasize play-based exploration. Children develop greater independence and begin to engage in collaborative activities with peers.

Program Highlights:

  • Enhanced language development and vocabulary building
  • Introduction to pre-literacy and pre-numeracy concepts
  • Social skills and cooperative play
  • Creative arts and expression
  • Physical activities to develop coordination and balance
  • Basic concept formation and cognitive skills

Junior KG (4-5 years)

  • Science exploration and environmental awareness
  • Enhanced fine motor skills for writing readiness
  • Social studies and cultural awareness
  • Problem-solving and critical thinking activities

Assessment and Progress Tracking

At Evergreen, we believe in continuous assessment to track each child's progress and tailor our approach to meet their individual needs. Our assessment methods include:

  • Observation-based assessment of skills and development
  • Portfolio of children's work and achievements
  • Regular parent-teacher meetings to discuss progress
  • Developmental checklists aligned with age-appropriate milestones
  • Termly progress reports highlighting strengths and areas for growth

Our goal is to provide meaningful feedback that celebrates each child's achievements while supporting their continued growth and development.
