Resources and information for all members of the Evergreen family

For Parents

At Evergreen, we believe that parents are the most important partners in a child's education. We provide various resources to help you stay involved in your child's learning journey:

  • Regular progress reports and parent-teacher meetings
  • Parent workshops and educational seminars
  • Access to curriculum updates and learning materials
  • Parent community events and networking opportunities
  • Digital portal for communication with teachers and staff

For Teachers

Our dedicated teachers are the heart of Evergreen. We provide comprehensive support and resources to help our educators excel:

  • Professional development opportunities and workshops
  • Curriculum resources and teaching materials
  • Collaborative planning tools and platforms
  • Mentorship programs for new teachers
  • Teacher wellness and support initiatives

For Students

Our students are at the center of everything we do. We provide a range of resources to support their learning and development:

  • Personalized learning support and resources
